DESeq

DESeq takes all transcripts with similar expression values into consideration. To determine overdispersion parameters in different conditions, it is assumed by DESeq that overdispersion is related to mean reads count. Differentially expressed genes are validated by an exact test between means of distribution parameters in two conditions. In this project, DESeq was last updated as DESeq2 (1.6.2).
